TOP PLACEMENTS

WHY SVGI

Established in 2004, as an institute of national importance, SVGI is recognized worldwide as a leader in the field of research and education in engineering and sciences. Our mission is to create an ambience in which new ideas and creativity flourish. The motto of SVGI is to provide learning blended with excellence, to create leaders of tomorrow. The dynamic and constantly evolving academic program reflects the institute's commitment to stay in tune with the expanding frontiers of knowledge worldwide. Extracurricular activities are also treated with equal importance towards overall development making the students at SVGI fit to take on the challenges faced in the competitive corporate world. Backed by the support of Alumni in different sectors and guidance of esteemed professors, we strive to offer highly nurturing environment to all its students.

Alumni

Our Alumni have emerged successful and excelled in varied professions across the globe. This network is highly enriching for the growth of our community.

Rankings

Recognized as India’s No. 1 University, we strive for excellence. Our rankings are reflective of our steep progress.

Selection Process

All the students enrolled at SVGI are selected after rigorous screening process. It ensures that we nurture India’s few most brilliant minds.

All round development

One’s skills, aptitude and perception reflect the personality of an individual. We offer numerous opportunities for multi dimensional growth of an individual.

MESSAGE FROM TNP CELL

The Training and Placement cell of SVGI is vibrant and strives to place the final year students of Diploma, B.Tech, Pharmacy and MBA. The training & placement cell is known for extending full time services to the students by providing proper guidance to get placed in campus interviews. The training and placement cell of SVGI is very scrupulous in terms of connectivity with leading companies and works assiduously for the training and placement of the students where the students are aided to go through the entire selection process.

To prepare the students for campus recruitment, the placement cell also arranges and conducts various aptitude tests, group discussions, and preparation sessions for technical and HR rounds of the interviews.

The best part of the placements of the group is that every student is encouraged to appear in as many interviews as they wish unless they succeed. The entire placement process is governed by the student's ability and performance, as well as the eligibility norms of the companies. The SVGI training & placement cell process aims to match the requirements of esteemed recruiters and the aspirations of students.
