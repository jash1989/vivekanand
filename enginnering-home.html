COURSES WE OFFER

OUR PROGRAMES

Diploma of Engineering/Diploma Course:

The Diploma in Engineering is a course that emphasizes hands-on, skill-based training. It seeks to provide students with fundamental technical knowledge, scientific expertise, computer and data analysis abilities, mathematical methodologies, a strong command of the English language to communicate in the field, and the capacity to apply problem-solving strategies. The course can be completed in two years after ITI and three years after 10th or 12th grade. There are several specialties of the engineering diploma degree, including mechanical, civil, and electrical engineering. In accordance with their interests and career objectives, students might select a specialism.


Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):

Students can prepare for a future in engineering by completing a four-year undergraduate degree programme called the Bachelor of Technology. Math, physics, engineering science, and engineering design are frequently taught in the programme. Also, students might choose to focus on a certain area of engineering disciplines including mechanical, electrical, civil, or chemical.


Master of Technology (M.Tech):

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) is a two-year graduate program that focuses on research. Students, who wish to work in academia or the engineering industry, conduct engineering research, or both are the intended audience for this program. The program covers advanced engineering subjects such as simulation and modeling, optimization and analysis of engineering.

OUR COLLABRATIONS

Collaborations in engineering are essential for the success of any project. They bring together individuals with diverse backgrounds and experiences, enabling the sharing of knowledge and expertise, promoting innovation, and enhancing problem-solving. While collaborations can be challenging, the benefits far outweigh the challenges, and effective collaborations can lead to improved productivity, reduced costs, and enhanced quality.
