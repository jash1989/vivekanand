DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

HOD- Civil Engineering Department

Mr. Goutam Verma

He is pursuing PhD in Civil Engineering with specialization in Structural Engineering from SRK University, Bhopal, M.P. He has completed his graduation (B.E. Civil Engg.) from SGSITS Indore. He has completed his post-graduation (M.E.) in Civil Engineering with specialization in Structural Engineering from SGSITS Indore. He has taken visiting lectures in IET, DAVV in Civil Engineering. He has total professional experience of 13 years in teaching industry. He has published 8 papers in various national/international journals. He has keen interest in structural engineering. He is Life member of Indian Geotechnical Society.

VISION

  • To impart knowledge and excellence in Civil Engineering and technology with global perspectives to our students and to make them ethically strong engineers to build our nation

MISSION

  • To develop highly competent professionals by giving them practical knowledge through Technical Trainings, Site Visits (National Level) and Equipment for Surveying, Testing etc.
  • To make them professional in drafting and designing by training them on software like AutoCAD, Staad-pro, Revit etc.
  • To prepare them for various positions in civil engineering, further study, life-long learning, and societal leadership by providing them mentorship through highly qualified faculties
    •

Program Educational Objectives

  • To provide the imperatives knowledge of science and engineering concepts fundamental for a computer professional and equip the proficiency of mathematical foundations and algorithmic principles for competent problem-solving ability
  • To exhibit leadership capability, triggering social and economic commitment and inculcate community services and protect the environment.
  • To train students with good scientific and engineering logics to comprehend, analyze, design and create novel products as well as solutions for the real time problems.

Program Specific Outcomes

  • To use mathematical methodologies to crack problems using suitable mathematical analysis, data structure and suitable algorithms.
  • The ability to interpret the fundamental concepts and methodology of computer systems. Students can understand the functionality of hardware and software aspects of computer systems.
  • The ability to grasp the software development lifecycle and methodologies of software systems.
