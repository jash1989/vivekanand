Dignitary Message

Americans rose to their feet on the address of Swami Vivekanand in Chicago. Today I urge the young Technocrats of Swami Vivekanand College of Engineering and Pharmacy to follow Swami Vivekanand's message to youth. "Arise awake and stop not till the goal is reached." Do the best in your chosen field. Achieve excellence.

Indian institutions are renowned world over. With dedication and commitment, SVCE can achieve the highest standard in education. I congratulate and wish the very best to students, teachers and members of management board of the Swami Vivekanand College of Engineering and Pharmacy.
