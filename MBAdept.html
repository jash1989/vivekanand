VISION

  • Our vision is to be a premier MBA program globally recognized for producing visionary leaders who drive impactful change in the business world.
  • We aim to cultivate a learning ecosystem that promotes excellence, encourages continuous growth, and embraces emerging trends.
  • By cultivating a culture of innovation, research, and real-world application, we aspire to shape the future of business and contribute to the betterment of society.

MISSION

  • The mission of an MBA department typically revolves around providing high-quality education and preparing students for successful careers in the business world.
  • The department is dedicated to delivering a rigorous and comprehensive curriculum that imparts cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills in various areas of business management.
  • The mission includes fostering the development of strong leadership qualities in students.
    •

Program Educational Objectives

  • Graduates of the MBA program will demonstrate the ability to advance in their careers by assuming leadership roles in diverse business environments.
  • Graduates will possess strong analytical and critical thinking skills, enabling them to identify complex business problems, evaluate potential solutions, and make informed decisions based on data and evidence.
  • Graduates will excel in communication, both oral and written, as well as exhibit strong interpersonal skills.

Program Specific Outcomes

  • Graduates will excel in communication, both oral and written, as well as exhibit strong interpersonal skills. They will be able to effectively collaborate with diverse teams, convey ideas persuasively, and engage in constructive dialogue within and beyond their organizations.
  • Graduates will recognize ethical dilemmas in business situations and apply ethical frameworks to make responsible decisions.
  • Graduates will embrace innovation and possess an entrepreneurial mindset.
